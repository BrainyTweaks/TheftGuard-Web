System Guide

Your complete guide to monitoring your grid and detecting power theft.

1. How Theft Detection Works

The system constantly compares the Source (power entering the grid) with the Load (power legitimately consumed by the house). If Source > Load by more than 1.0A, a Critical Red Alert banner triggers instantly, indicating a potential bypass or physical line tap.

2. Dual Analytics (Load vs. Source)

The dashboard features two independent charts. The top blue chart maps your House Load, while the bottom yellow chart maps the Grid Source. Scroll down to compare the two.

  • Live: Real-time minute-by-minute stream directly from your hardware.
  • Hour: Zoom into a specific 60-minute window for granular analysis.
  • Day/Week/Month/Year: View historical aggregated data.
3. Cost & Budgeting

Navigate to the Cost tab to view your estimated monthly bill. Total Cost is calculated as: Units Consumed (kWh) × Rate per Unit (₹). You can adjust the unit rate dynamically to match your local utility pricing.

4. Hardware & Settings

Click Pair Device in the top navigation bar to link your ESP32 MAC address. Use the gear icon next to your username to manage your account settings, including username changes and password resets.
